› About Ankit


Ankit Duseja is a tech enthusiast, developer, author and entrepreneur who has been freelancing since his school days. He holds a master's degree in computer applications from VIT University, Vellore and has worked as STO with Times Internet Ltd. (also referred as Bennett, Coleman and Co. Ltd.) for their product TimesofIndia.com. He had also freelanced for many individuals and companies which includes a U.S. based medical equipment firm named FootLeveler's Inc, where he developed a complete medical analysis and reporting software for multiple platforms. The other projects are some of his research publications which he carried out while pursuing his master's degree at VIT University.


Ankit's primary interest is in web technologies, though his projects also involve researching modern UI interfaces, cloud collaboration and artificial intelligence.
